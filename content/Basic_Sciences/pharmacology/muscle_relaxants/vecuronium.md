Dose: **0.1 mg/kg**  
Maintenance: **0.01-0.05 mg/kg**   
Onset: **2 - 3 minutes**  
Peak: **2.5 minutes**   
Duration: **25 - 50 minutes**  
Elimination: **Some Hepatic Metabolism, Excretion 40% Biliary/30% Renal**  
Mechanism of Action: **Nicotinic M receptor antagonist (competitive inhibition)**  


- Final concentration (requires reconstitution) = 1 mg/ml
- Monoquartenary aminosteroid
- Nonvagolytic as compared to other aminosteroid NMBs
- Acceptable for renal patients
- Prolonged duration with hepatic disease
