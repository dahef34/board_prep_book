Dose: **0.2 mg**  
Onset: **60 seconds**  
Peak: **1 - 3 minutes**  
Duration: **45 - 90 minutes**  
Elimination: **Hepatic**  
Mechanism of Action: **GABA antagonist**  

- Repeat doses can range from 0.2-0.5 mg every minute
- Most patients respond at 0.6 - 1 mg
- Max total dose 3 mg
- Monitor for recrudescence
